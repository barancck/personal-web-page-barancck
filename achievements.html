Most Creative Technology Award

On May 19, 2023, Baran Çiçek's project, FIORES GLOBAL, one of the early stage entrepreneur candidates of Hacettepe IDEAL, was awarded the “Most Creative Technology Award” by Enterprise Azerbaijan. The award was given as part of the StartUp School Project, where FIORES GLOBAL was selected among 200 projects from 7 different countries. FIORES GLOBAL is a technology project focused on 3D imaging systems in the field of health technologies.
